What we do

At Brothers Bait, our reputation precedes us for our exceptional ability to catch large fish. Our expertise lies in a select few species, including Musky, Walleye, Smallmouth, Trout, and Salmon. As we fish daily, we stay updated on the latest hot bait and prime locations. Our confidence in our products leads us to exclusively use our own baits and rods. We go beyond simply guiding our customers by teaching them how to use our gear and offering advice on when to use certain items. Despite being a small operation run by two brothers, we are capable of taking both single individuals and large groups on our guided fishing trips. To showcase our fishing adventures, we maintain an active presence on Instagram and Facebook. Our services are available year-round, ensuring that you can book us anytime.
